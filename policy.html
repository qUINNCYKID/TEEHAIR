Deposit & Payment

  • ◆ A $15 confirmation payment is required for all full services ($5 for wash only). No deposit = No appointment.
  • ◆ Accepted payments: Zelle, CashApp, Apple Pay, or Cash.
  • ◆ All remaining balances must be paid in full before the completion of your service.
timer_off

Late & Cancellations

  • ◆ Grace Period: 10-15 minutes. A $10 late fee applies after 10 mins, increasing to $20 at 15 mins.
  • ◆ Notice: 24-48 hours notice is required for rescheduling or cancellation. Deposits are non-refundable for same-day changes or no-shows.
  • ◆ Appointments are automatically cancelled after 30 minutes of delay.
person_cancel

Prep & Guest Policy

  • ◆ Please arrive with clean hair. An extra fee will be applied for excessive product buildup. A blow dry is included in all services.
  • ◆ No extra guests are permitted in the salon area.
  • ◆ Travel Service: A $30 travel fee option is available specifically for clients with children who cannot leave home.

Health & Media

  • ◆ If you are feeling unwell or have symptoms, please reschedule. We reserve the right to refuse service to ensure safety.
  • ◆ Marketing photos/videos may be taken during your session. Please inform your stylist if you wish to opt-out.
  • Note: The exact location for your appointment will be sent via text/email the day prior to your scheduled time.
